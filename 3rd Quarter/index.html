HOW TO PLAY

Guess the word before the character is fully drawn

Click on a letter on the keyboard to guess if the letter is in the word or not. If it is, you got a letter right. Keep going. If it isn't, you got a letter wrong and the mistake counter will go up. The more mistakes you make, the more parts of the characters is drawn.

Once you make 6 mistakes, the character will be fully drawn which will constitute to a loss. However, if you guess the word correctly before you hit 6 mistakes, you win.

Have fun playing!
